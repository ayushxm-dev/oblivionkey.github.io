Quantum PNT & Applied Cryptography

Ayush Meshram

Junior Research Fellow (JRF), Department of Information Technology, National Institute of Technology Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India

I work on privacy-preserving systems and applied cryptography—with interest in multivariate crypto, zero-knowledge proofs, PKI, and PQC—and on post-quantum signatures and secure timing for GNSS-denied settings, building prototypes in Rust, C/C++, and Go with formal security validation. As Junior Research Fellow at NIT Raipur, I lead design and development of a quantum-based PNT (positioning, navigation, and timing) system for underwater robot navigation.
